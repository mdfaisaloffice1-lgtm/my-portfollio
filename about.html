MY STORY

Back in 2021, I was frustrated. I kept seeing Shopify stores loaded with expensive apps that slowed everything down and drained merchants' budgets month after month. I thought, "There has to be a better way."

That's when I made a decision that changed everything: I would master custom Liquid code and build solutions that apps couldn't match. No monthly fees. No bloat. Just clean, fast, custom code that does exactly what the client needs.

The first few projects were challenging. I spent countless late nights debugging, learning Shopify's architecture inside-out, and perfecting my craft. But with each store I built, I got better. Faster. More creative.

Five years later, I've delivered 250+ high-performance Shopify stores to clients across the globe.
