MY JOURNEY

From freelance beginner to 250+ successful Shopify stores. This is my story of hard work, learning, and dedication to custom code excellence.

In 2021, I started as a complete beginner in e-commerce development. Like many developers, I was frustrated seeing businesses struggle with expensive, bloated apps that promised the world but delivered slow loading times and monthly subscription nightmares.

I had a simple idea: What if stores didn't need all those apps? What if everything could be built with clean, native Liquid code? It seemed impossible at first, but I was determined to prove it could work.

I spent countless nights learning Shopify's Liquid templating language, JavaScript, and API integrations. Every rejection taught me something new. Every bug I solved made me stronger. I built my first custom feature to replace a $29/month app, and the client was thrilled.

Word spread quickly. From Germany to Singapore, from small startups to established brands, businesses started reaching out. They were tired of paying app fees. They wanted speed, control, and custom solutions that actually worked.

Today, after 250+ successful projects, I've saved my clients over $2.4 million in app subscription fees. But more importantly, I've helped businesses take full control of their Shopify stores with custom code that performs better, costs less, and gives them complete ownership.
