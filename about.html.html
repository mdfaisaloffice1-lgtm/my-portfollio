MY JOURNEY

From learning Shopify in 2021 to delivering 250+ stores across 15 countries. This is my story of hard work, manual coding, and helping businesses succeed.

THE BEGINNING

In 2021, I discovered Shopify and immediately saw its potential. While others relied on paid apps and quick fixes, I took a different path — I learned to code everything from scratch in Liquid.

I spent countless nights studying Shopify's documentation, building test stores, and perfecting my craft. My goal was simple: create solutions that were faster, cheaper, and better than any app on the market.

GOING GLOBAL

My reputation grew organically. A German fashion brand found me through word-of-mouth. Their store was slow, bloated with apps, and losing conversions. I replaced 12 apps with custom code and increased their revenue by 150%.

Soon, I was working with clients from Singapore, USA, UK, Germany, and across Asia. Each project taught me something new. Each challenge made me better.
