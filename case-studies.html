THE SPEED RESCUE

From 8.2s to 1.9s Load Time

A German fashion retailer was losing customers fast. Their store took 8.2 seconds to load, and during flash sales, it would crash completely. They had 23 paid apps—each one making the problem worse.

I removed all apps and rebuilt every feature with custom Liquid code. The result? Load time dropped to 1.9 seconds, and they handled their biggest flash sale ever—10,000 concurrent users—without a single crash.

What I Did:

  • Removed 23 apps, rebuilt functionality in pure Liquid
  • Implemented lazy loading and deferred JavaScript
  • Custom caching system for high-traffic events

Results:

Conversion rate jumped from 1.2% to 4.8%. Cart abandonment dropped 67%. App costs went from $847/month to $0.

THE PAYMENT REVOLUTION

From 23% to 2% Cart Abandonment

A UK luxury home décor brand had a nightmare checkout. Customers had to leave Shopify, complete payment elsewhere, then return. 23 out of 100 customers abandoned their carts.

I integrated Stripe, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay with seamless one-page checkout. Cart abandonment dropped to just 2%, and mobile conversions tripled overnight.

Integration:

  • Seamless Stripe & PayPal one-page checkout
  • Apple Pay & Google Pay for mobile users
  • Custom fraud detection and prevention

Impact:

Cart abandonment: 23% → 2%. Transaction fees saved: $892/month. Mobile conversions tripled. £127,000 additional revenue in Q1.
