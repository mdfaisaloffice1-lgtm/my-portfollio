THE SPEED RESCUE

From 8.2s to 1.9s Load Time

A fashion retailer from Germany contacted me in desperation. Their Shopify store was crawling at 8.2 seconds load time, and customers were abandoning carts faster than they could blink. They had installed 23 paid apps trying to solve various problems, but each one made the site slower.

The worst part? During flash sales, the site would crash completely. They were losing thousands in revenue every week, and their Google Ads were draining budget on visitors who left before the page even loaded.

What I Did:

  • Removed all 23 apps and rebuilt their functionality with custom Liquid code
  • Implemented lazy loading for images and deferred JavaScript execution
  • Optimized database queries and removed redundant Metafield calls
  • Created custom caching system for high-traffic flash sales

THE PAYMENT REVOLUTION

From 23% to 2% Cart Abandonment

A luxury home decor brand in the UK was bleeding revenue. Their checkout process was a nightmare—customers had to leave Shopify, complete payment on a third-party gateway, then return for confirmation. The friction was unbearable.

Out of every 100 customers who added items to cart, 23 abandoned at checkout. The worst part? Their payment gateway charged 3.5% + $0.30 per transaction, and customers complained about security concerns with the redirect process. They were losing both money and trust.

The Complete Overhaul:

  • Integrated Stripe and PayPal with seamless one-page checkout experience
  • Added Apple Pay and Google Pay for mobile shoppers (65% of their traffic)
  • Implemented custom fraud detection system using Liquid and webhook automation
  • Created real-time payment confirmation and instant order processing workflow
