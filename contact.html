FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick answers to common concerns

How fast can you complete a project?

It depends on the complexity. Simple bug fixes and app replacements: 1-3 days. Medium complexity projects (custom features, automation): 1-2 weeks. Full store builds or major overhauls: 3-6 weeks. I always provide a realistic timeline upfront—no surprises.

Will my store be down during the work?

Never. I work on a duplicate theme and test everything thoroughly before going live. Your store stays operational 24/7. Deployment happens during your lowest traffic hours to minimize any potential impact.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes. Every project includes 30 days of free bug fixes and support. After that, you can opt for monthly maintenance packages starting at $297/month, which includes updates, monitoring, and priority support.

How do you ensure my store stays fast after removing apps?

I write optimized Liquid code that's native to Shopify—no external dependencies. This means faster load times, better SEO, and complete control. I also implement caching strategies, lazy loading, and code minification to maintain peak performance.

What if I need changes after the project is done?

Minor tweaks during the 30-day support period are free. For new features or major changes, I provide transparent hourly rates or project quotes. Most of my clients become long-term partners because they trust my work and pricing.

Can you work with my existing developer or agency?

Absolutely. I'm happy to collaborate with your team or step in for specific tasks. I provide clean, well-documented code and clear communication to ensure smooth teamwork.

Do you sign NDAs or confidentiality agreements?

Yes, if required. Your business confidentiality and data security are paramount. I'm comfortable signing NDAs and following your security protocols.

What payment methods do you accept?

I accept bank transfers, PayPal, Stripe, and cryptocurrency for international clients. Payment terms are typically 50% upfront and 50% upon completion for new clients, with flexible arrangements for ongoing partnerships.
