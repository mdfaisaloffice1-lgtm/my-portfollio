FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Get answers to common concerns about Shopify development

Will my store slow down without apps?

Actually, the opposite! Apps are the #1 cause of slow Shopify stores. Custom Liquid code is lightweight, optimized, and loads 3-5x faster than app-based solutions. You'll see immediate speed improvements.

What if I need changes later?

I provide ongoing maintenance packages and quick turnaround for modifications. Since you own the code, you're never locked into a subscription or dependent on an app company.

Is custom development more expensive upfront?

While there's an initial investment, you save money long-term. Most stores pay $300-800/month in app fees. Custom solutions pay for themselves in 2-4 months and then save you thousands annually.

Can you work with existing stores or just new ones?

I work with both! I can audit your existing store, identify bottlenecks, remove unnecessary apps, and optimize your current setup. No need to start from scratch.

What about security and updates?

All code follows Shopify's best practices and security standards. Unlike apps that can have vulnerabilities, custom code is more secure and doesn't require constant updates from third parties.

Do you provide documentation?

Absolutely! Every project includes comprehensive documentation, code comments, and training videos so you or your team can understand and maintain the solutions.
