How I replaced 12 paid apps with custom code and tripled conversion rates

THE PROBLEM

A mid-sized German fashion brand came to me frustrated. Their store was drowning in apps — 12 different subscriptions costing them €650/month. The site loaded in 8.2 seconds, they were losing 65% of visitors before the page even loaded, and their checkout had a 40% abandonment rate.

THE SOLUTION

I conducted a complete audit and replaced every single app with custom Liquid code:

  • Reviews app → Native review system with schema markup
